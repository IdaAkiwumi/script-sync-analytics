**Script-Sync Analytics** is a narrative ROI engine designed for Studio Executives, Creative Producers, and Narrative Strategists. While writers focus on the *art* of the script, Script-Sync focuses on the *viability*, transforming raw movie datasets into actionable product roadmaps.

---

### ✨ Why Script-Sync Analytics?

In a high-stakes production environment, "gut feeling" isn't enough to greenlight a project. **Script-Sync** provides the analytical rigor required for strategic creative expansion by providing:

* **Sentiment ROI Mapping:** Uses NLP (Natural Language Processing) to score narrative themes against historical market success.
* **Genre Friction Analysis:** Visualizes market saturation vs. audience appetite, identifying "Blue Ocean" opportunities for original IP.
* **The UX:** Built by a **Lead Product Designer**, the dashboard prioritizes WCAG 2.1 accessibility and high-contrast data storytelling for rapid executive decision-making.
* **Predictive Roadmap Logic:** Automatically flags narrative archetypes—like "Legal Thriller" or "Action Comedy"—that are currently trending in global industry datasets.

---

### 🛠️ Tech Stack & Key Features
* **Data Visualization:** Powered by `Plotly` and `Pandas` for interactive, deep-dive scatter plots.
* **NLP Engine:** Integrated `TextBlob` logic to quantify the emotional resonance of narrative datasets.
* **Strategic UI:** Custom Streamlit components designed for "Manager View" metrics.

---

### 🔍 Industry Use Cases
* **Studio Executives:** Use "Market Appetite" metrics to validate greenlight decisions.
* **Creative Ops:** Map out the "Predictive Roadmap" for the next fiscal year based on narrative gaps.
* **Narrative Strategists:** Prove the "Emotional ROI" of a script to stakeholders using hard data.
