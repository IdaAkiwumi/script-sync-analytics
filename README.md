**Script Sync Analytics** is a narrative ROI (Return on Investment) engine designed for Studio Executives, Creative Producers, and Narrative Strategists. While writers focus on the *art* of the script, Script Sync focuses on the *viability*, transforming raw movie datasets into actionable product roadmaps.

---

### 📈 Intelligence Metrics Defined

To provide a high-level "Manager View," the dashboard calculates three proprietary metrics derived from a dataset of 1M+ movie records:

* **Avg Sentiment ROI (Return on Investment):** This uses NLP (Natural Language Processing) to measure the "emotional resonance" of a genre. It scores how historically well certain story themes connect with audiences. A **High** score suggests a strong emotional "hit rate" for that category.
* **Market Appetite:** This represents the current "hunger" or popularity for a genre. Instead of a simple average, this metric looks at the **90th percentile (top performers)** to show the potential "ceiling" for a high-quality project in today’s market.
* **Genre Friction:** This measures market saturation. 
    * **Low Friction:** An "Open Door" or **Blue Ocean** opportunity where few competitors exist, allowing your project to stand out easily.
    * **High Friction:** A "Red Ocean" or crowded market where many similar projects are already competing for attention.

### ✨ Why Script Sync Analytics?

In a high-stakes production environment, "gut feeling" isn't enough to greenlight a project. **Script Sync** provides the analytical rigor required for strategic creative expansion by providing:

* **Sentiment ROI Mapping:** Uses NLP logic to score narrative themes against historical market success.
* **Genre Friction Analysis:** Visualizes market saturation vs. audience appetite, identifying "Blue Ocean" opportunities for original IP (Intellectual Property).
* **The UX:** Built by a **Lead Product Designer**, the dashboard prioritizes WCAG 2.1 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) and high-contrast data storytelling for rapid executive decision-making.
* **Predictive Roadmap Logic:** Automatically flags narrative archetypes—like "Legal Thriller" or "Action Comedy"—that are currently trending in global industry datasets.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack & Key Features
* **Data Visualization:** Powered by `Plotly` and `Pandas` for interactive, deep-dive scatter plots.
